About

It is a rap group that was founded by Acarkhan in 2009 and released their first underground album in 2010. When the group was first formed, there were 6 people, but later on, due to some personal problems, Maxim and KC left the group.

In 2011, They sold the car they won from a competition of a navigation company and opened a high-end studio. The studio was closed in 2013 and the band took a break from making music for a while.
